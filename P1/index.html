cyworld

Cyworld is a Korean social network service. It has a cyber currency called acorns. People reveal their personality through minimis, minirooms and mini homepages that show their tastes. People can buy items for decorating thier minirooms or minimis by acorns. Also, they can buy music to use as background music for thier mini hompages with acorns.
